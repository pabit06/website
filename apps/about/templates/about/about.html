About Bhanjyang

Our Identity. Our Purpose.

Building a stronger community through financial empowerment and member-centric services since 2012.

Our Foundation

Rooted in Values

Bhanjyang Saving & Credit Cooperative Ltd. was established in 2012 with the vision of providing accessible financial solutions to the residents of Rupa Rural Municipality. For over a decade, we have been a trusted partner in the economic development of our community.

Our cooperative operates on the principle of mutual help and democratic control. We ensure that every member has a voice and that our growth directly benefits the community we serve.

Our Mission

To empower our members by providing sustainable, transparent, and innovative financial services while fostering a culture of savings and investment.

Our Vision

To be the leading financial institution in the region, known for its integrity, member satisfaction, and contribution to social prosperity.

Cooperative Identity

The 7 Principles

As a registered cooperative, we strictly adhere to the International Cooperative Principles.

1. Voluntary Membership

Open to all persons able to use their services and willing to accept the responsibilities of membership.

2. Democratic Control

Controlled by their members, who actively participate in setting their policies and making decisions.

3. Member Participation

Members contribute equitably to, and democratically control, the capital of their cooperative.

4. Autonomy

Self-help organizations controlled by their members, ensuring independence in all partnerships.

5. Education & Training

Providing education for members and training for representatives to contribute to development.

6. Cooperation

Serving members most effectively and strengthening the cooperative movement by working together.

7. Community Concern

Working for the sustainable development of their communities through policies approved by members.
